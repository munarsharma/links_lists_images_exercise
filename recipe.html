The perfect Zait oo Za'atar sandwich

Description

A Zait oo Za'atar sandwich is best enjoyed in the morning with a hot cup of sweetened mint tea.

Ingredients

  • 1 freshly baked pita bread
  • 5 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of za’atar
  • Sesame seeds to sprinkle
  • Optional: feta cheese

Directions

You can make this yummy sandwich two ways. I prefer option 1.

  1. Place pita bread in a toaster until it is hot, but not hot enough to burn you
  2. Option 1: Slice pita in half to create two pita pockets. Option 2: Leave pita as is.
  3. Place pita bread in a toaster until it is hot, but not hot enough to burn you.
  4. Option 1: Slice pita in half to create two pita pockets. Option 2: Leave pita as is
  5. In a small bowl, mix in the za’atar and olive oil to make a za’atar paste
  6. Option 1: spread the za’atar paste evenly into the pita pockets and add sesame seeds. Option 2: Spread the za’atar paste on top of the pitta bread and add sesame seeds.. You may need to add extra olive oil here if the paste is too thick.
  7. Optional: spread a generous amount of feta cheese onto of the za’atar paste for a more tangy flavor
  8. Place the sandwich in the oven again for about 3 minutes
